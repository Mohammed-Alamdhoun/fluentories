About Fluentory

Fluentory is a skills-focused language platform built to boost real-world confidence through practical, grammar-light learning. Founded in Amman in 2020, it offers personalized coaching, resume support, mock interviews, and more, all designed to prepare learners for global success. While we began with Business English, Fluentories is expanding to include other languages soon, because communication shouldn't have borders.

What We Offer You

  • Business English Courses Build professional fluency for interviews, meetings, and global careers with a practical, grammar-light approach.
  • Kids’ Public Speaking Programs Develop children's confidence, communication, and presentation skills through fun, interactive training.
  • 1-on-1 Language Coaching Personalized sessions tailored to your specific goals—whether for career growth, education, or public speaking.
  • Resume & Interview Preparation Sharpen your professional profile with expert CV feedback and mock interviews designed for real-world success.
  • Multilingual Courses (Coming Soon) Fluentories is expanding to offer courses in new languages—because effective communication goes beyond English.

What We Offer

Vocabulary & Communication

We provide you with a 500-word booklet full of workplace expressions and phrases, helping you speak naturally, improve your fluency, and communicate confidently in real-world professional environments.

Career-Focused Learning

You’ll receive curated recommendations for free, high-impact courses that align with your career goals — helping you strengthen both your technical and soft skills to stand out in the job market.

Interview Preparation

We simulate real job interviews based on the company and position you’re targeting, then give you actionable feedback to improve your answers, body language, and confidence before the real thing.

CV Optimization

Your CV is your first impression — we help you structure it professionally, refine the language, highlight key strengths, and make sure it’s clear, polished, and completely free from mistakes.
